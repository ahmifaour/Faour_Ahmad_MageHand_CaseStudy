Welcome to the Mage-Hand Repository!

Welcome to the Mage Hand Repository! This website is designed to tackle the outdated need for physical character sheets when playing Dungeons and Dragons (5e only currently). Tired of printing out character sheets? Dreading taking your binder with you to game night? If so, then this is the website for you! Begin your adventure with a virtual character sheet, and delve through the dungeon of our various databases! Unfortunately, we do not currently have a character generator but we will have one in the future. For now though, you can still create a virtual character sheet and import your stats (instead of having to reprint that darn sheet and fill it out again)!
